Gameplay

More than a puzzle. A survival network.

Every settlement depends on your signal chain. Design modular relay stations, adapt to dynamic weather, and keep the network alive against the elements.

📡

Realistic Signal Propagation

Radio signals follow line-of-sight physics. Terrain blocks, distance attenuates, and weather disrupts. Build multi-hop relay chains to reach isolated settlements.

🔧

Modular Equipment System

Configure each station with 5 component types — transmitters, antennas, power sources, housing, and frequency modules. Every combination creates a different strategy.

Dynamic Weather & Day/Night

Thunderstorms triple signal attenuation. Solar panels die at night. Wind turbines thrive in storms. Adapt your network to survive every condition.

Environments

Four biomes. Four new challenges.

Each environment changes how you build, plan, and survive. Unique terrain, weather patterns, and visual design across every biome.

🏔️

Alpine

High ridges and broken valleys. Snow-covered peaks block signals. Mountain winds test your equipment at every turn.

🏜️

Desert

Heat shimmer and exposed mesas. Sand dunes swallow signals. Solar panels thrive by day but leave you dark at night.

🌊

Coastal

Salt air, tides, and brittle cliffs. Fog banks disrupt transmissions. Balance deep-water crossings with fragile shoreline routes.

❄️

Tundra

Frozen ground under thin light. Ice storms cripple unprotected stations. The most demanding environment for any operator.
