Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Summary: Signal Lost does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app works entirely offline and requires no account or internet connection to play.

1. Data We Collect

None. Signal Lost does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or telemetry of any kind. We have no servers, no databases, and no way to receive data from your device.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

Signal Lost stores the following data locally on your device only, using Apple's built-in SwiftData framework and UserDefaults:

  • Your player name and gameplay progress (campaign maps completed, star ratings)
  • Equipment unlocks and resource inventory
  • Daily challenge scores and history
  • Story fragments collected during gameplay
  • App settings (audio volume, haptics, colorblind mode, reduced motion)

This data never leaves your device. It is not synced to any server, cloud service, or third party. Deleting the app removes all stored data.

3. Third-Party Services

Signal Lost integrates with Apple Game Center for achievements and leaderboards. Game Center is operated entirely by Apple and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Game Center use is optional and controlled by your device settings.

No other third-party services, SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or tracking tools are used in this app.

4. Advertising & Tracking

Signal Lost contains no advertisements and performs no tracking of any kind. We do not use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA), fingerprinting, or any other tracking mechanism.

5. In-App Purchases

Signal Lost is a one-time purchase with no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or microtransactions.

6. Children's Privacy

Since Signal Lost collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages. We comply with COPPA, GDPR-K, and all applicable children's privacy regulations by default — there is simply no data to protect.

7. Data Deletion

To delete all data associated with Signal Lost, simply delete the app from your device. Since no data is transmitted or stored externally, uninstalling the app is a complete data deletion.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Given the app's zero-data-collection design, significant changes are unlikely.
